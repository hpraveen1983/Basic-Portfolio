About Me

The Enterprise Application Technology Lead partners with academic and business units within the University to understand business need, implement new systems and support solutions to help meet their objectives. This position provides expert technical guidance to others within the Application Support team across all application support functions. Provides advanced technical expertise to the Application Support team and works closely with multiple internal teams across disparate groups to resolve client support issues Provides expert level implementation, support and maintenance of on-premises and cloud based enterprise applications at the server and desktop level including, but not limited to; application installs and upgrades, integration, maintenance, security administration, problem resolution, documentation, testing and end user training Participates in development of RFPs, feasibility studies, review of vendor contracts and SLAs
